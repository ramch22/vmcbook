VMware CloudGetting Started HowTo:Billing

Verify Billing

Billing is managed through the Billing interface of the cloud console.

  1. Click on the Services icon from within the cloud console.
  2. Click on Billing.

Manage Payment Methods

There must be a default payment method for VMware Cloud services. Verify this from the Manage Payment Methods screen of the Billing interface.

If you do not have a default payment method then you must link to funding through a My VMware account or provide a credit card for payment.

Activate Promotional Credits

Promotional credits may be applied as a payment method. Verify credits from the Promotional Credits screen of the Billing interface.

You must activate the credits before they may be used for payment.

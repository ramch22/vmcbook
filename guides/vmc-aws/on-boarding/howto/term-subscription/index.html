On-Boarding HowTo: Term Subscription

Create a Term Subscription

Term subscriptions are managed from within the vmc console. Click on “Create Subscription” to create a term subscription.

  1. Select the host type, host count, and region. Note that these values are permanent for the life of the subscription. In other words, you may not transfer a subscription between AWS regions or change the type or number of hosts.
  2. Choose a term. The discount varies per the term. 3.Confirm the subscription.
